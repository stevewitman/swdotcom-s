Personal Profile

I fell in love with programming as a teenager when I taught myself to write programs/games in BASIC. Since then, I have experimented with ASP, Visual Basic, VB.net, Java, Android, jQuery Mobile, and PHP. I also created a complete website for a ski shop that I worked for, with an online reservation system using ASP and MS Access database. I have previously taken classes in Java, Assembly, and Computer Architecture and have used LISP to write custom functions for AutoCAD in my work as a civil and structural designer/drafter. I am a gSchool/Galvanize Full Stack graduate (2015) - Ruby/Rails, JavaScript, Angular, Ember, jQuery, CSS, HTML5. Most evenings I still find myself digging into some code.

I am the organizer of Scratch Boulder, SketchUp Boulder and Free Code Camp - Boulder Study Group meetups and I am also a co-organizer of Boulder Ruby Group.

Before moving to Boulder, I lived in the mountains of Colorado for several years between Winter Park and Silverthorne. I am an avid skier and mountain biker. Feel free to contact me if you want to go skiing, mountain biking, or code on a project together.
